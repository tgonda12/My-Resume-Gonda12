Different types of skiing:

  1. Cross Country
    • Skiing long distances
    • Heels lift up from skis for walking purposes
    • Great way to hike in the snow at a faster rate
    • Great social experience
  2. Downhill Skiing
    • Many different skill levels
    • Racing
    • Freeride
    • Terrain Park Skiing
    • Big variety of size and flexibility of skis for different purposes
    • More dangerous then other types
  3. Telemark Skiing
    • Mix between Cross Country and Downhill
    • Heels not attached to skis
    • Involves full lunges to make turns down the hill
    • Less common
    • Great for back country skiing
    • Also a big variety of ski size and flexibility
